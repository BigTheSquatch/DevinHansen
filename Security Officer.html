Security Officer

I left MercyOne due to security and safety concerns. Management provided inadiquate equipment and information, that would/could eventually lead to legal issues and employee injury.

Duties at MercyOne as a Security Officer we're as follows:

  • Write daily activity logs and reports.
  • Work with law enforcement for applicable patients (court orders, suicidal, in custody, etc.).
  • Maintaining multiple annual certifications.
    • Mandt.
    • Mandatory Reporting (Child, dependent adult).
    • Basic Life Support (American Heart Association).
  • Responding to radio calls for assistance.
    • Patient/visitor Violence.
    • Facility/grounds issues.
    • Jump starts, accidents on grounds, unsecured vehicles.
    • Lost items.
    • Patient/visitor escorts.
    • Adverse weather.
  • Patrol Hospital grounds and satellite properties.
  • Secure the helicopter pad during takeoff/landing.
  • Monitor construction areas for fire hazards and break-ins
  • Covid-19 Screening
  • Maintain security equipment (pagers, uniform, vehicle)
